## IIT Madras BS Degree in Data Science and Applications: Fees Structure

The IIT Madras BS Degree in Data Science and Applications has a modular, pay-as-you-go fee structure. Students pay for only the courses they register for each term, allowing flexibility in financial planning. The total program cost depends on the number of credits and the pace at which a student progresses.

---

### **Summary of Total Fees by Level**

| Level | Number of Credits | Typical Total Fee (INR) |
| :-- | :-- | :-- |
| Foundation | 32 | ‚Çπ32,000 |
| Diploma Level | 54 (2 Diplomas) | ‚Çπ1,25,000 |
| BSc Degree Level | ~28 | ‚Çπ2.21‚Äì2.47 lakh |
| BS Degree Level | ~28 | ‚Çπ3.25‚Äì3.87 lakh |

*Note: The total fee for the full BS degree (all levels) is typically between ‚Çπ3.25 lakh and ‚Çπ3.87 lakh, depending on elective choices and pace of study. The BSc degree (without BS level) is typically between ‚Çπ2.21 lakh and ‚Çπ2.47 lakh.*[^1]

---

### **Detailed Fee Breakdown by Level**

#### **1. Foundation Level**

- **Credits:** 32
- **Fee per Credit:** ‚Çπ1,000
- **Total Fee:** ‚Çπ32,000


#### **2. Diploma Level**

- **Diploma in Programming \& Diploma in Data Science**
    - **Theory Course Fee:** ‚Çπ2,500 per credit
    - **Project Course Fee:** ‚Çπ1,250 per credit
    - **Total for Both Diplomas:** ‚Çπ1,25,000


#### **3. BSc Degree Level**

- **Level 3 Courses:** ‚Çπ2,500 per credit
- **Level 4 Courses:** ‚Çπ5,000 per credit
- **NPTEL Electives:** ‚Çπ1,000 per credit (for credit transfer)
- **Typical Range:** ‚Çπ2.21‚Äì2.47 lakh


#### **4. BS Degree Level**

- **Level 3 Courses:** ‚Çπ2,500 per credit
- **Level 4 Courses:** ‚Çπ5,000 per credit
- **NPTEL Electives:** ‚Çπ1,000 per credit (for credit transfer)
- **Typical Range:** ‚Çπ3.25‚Äì3.87 lakh

---

### **Other Fees and Charges**

- **Qualifier Exam Fee:** ‚Çπ3,000 (non-refundable; waivers available for SC/ST/PwD categories)
- **Re-attempt Fee for Qualifier Exam:** ‚Çπ1,500 (General/OBC), ‚Çπ750 (SC/ST/PwD), ‚Çπ375 (SC/ST \& PwD)
- **Project Course Registration:** ‚Çπ2,500 per project (with some waivers)
- **Makeup Exam Fee:** ‚Çπ1,000 per Foundation course, ‚Çπ2,000 per Diploma/Degree course
- **Repeat for Improvement:** Double the normal course fee
- **Registration Keep Alive Fee (RKA):** ‚Çπ1,000 per term (if not registering for any courses in a term)
- **Readmission Fee:** ‚Çπ2,500 (if struck off the rolls and seeking reactivation)
- **Dropping a Course:** Deduction of ‚Çπ2,000 (Foundation) or ‚Çπ4,000 (Diploma/Degree) per dropped course from refund
- **Credit Transfer from NPTEL:** ‚Çπ1,000 per credit
- **Credit Transfer from IITM Campus Courses:** ‚Çπ2,500 per credit (plus institute fees)
- **Apprenticeship Credit Transfer:** ‚Çπ5,000 per credit

---

### **Fee Waivers**

Fee waivers are available based on category and family income:

- **General/OBC:** Full fee unless family income < ‚Çπ5 lakh (50% waiver)
- **EWS/OBC-NCL:** 50% waiver (income < ‚Çπ5 lakh), 75% waiver (income < ‚Çπ1 lakh)
- **SC/ST/PwD:** 50% waiver (all), 75% waiver (income < ‚Çπ1 lakh)

*Documentation is required for availing waivers. See the student handbook for the latest details.*[^1]

---

### **International Students**

- **Additional Exam Facilitation Fee:**
    - Quiz 1: ‚Çπ2,000
    - Quiz 2: ‚Çπ2,000
    - End-term: ‚Çπ2,000 (one course) or ‚Çπ4,000 (multiple courses)
    - Fees may vary by country (e.g., ‚Çπ3,000 per session in Kuwait/Bahrain)[^1]

---

### **How Fees Are Paid**

- **Per Course, Per Term:** Students register and pay only for the courses they choose each term.
- **No Lump Sum:** There is no upfront lump-sum payment for the entire program.
- **Flexible Exit:** Students can exit after any level (Foundation, Diploma, BSc, BS) and pay only for completed courses.

---

### **Key Points**

- The program is designed to be affordable and flexible.
- Fee waivers make it accessible to students from various backgrounds.
- Students pay as they progress, reducing financial risk.

*For the most current and detailed fee tables, always refer to the official IIT Madras BS Degree Programme Student Handbook.*[^1]
